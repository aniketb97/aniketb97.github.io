About

Hello World! I am a CS Graduate student at the University of California, Davis. I am currently working as a Graduate Student Researcher for the Center for Regional Change under the guidance of Dr. Catherine Brinkley, Dr. Lindsay Poirier and Dr. Seth Frey.

Before starting my Master's journey, I was a Research Engineer at MAKERS LAB, Tech Mahindra Ltd. with my focus being on utilizing disruptive technologies to provide practical solutions in the AgriTech domain. Critical thinking, problem-solving, brainstorming innovative solutions, and communicating them to colleagues and clients were a daily part of my job.

I am always excited to learn new things and I quickly adapt to a new environment. I am good at context switching between parallel tasks. I have experience in applied Machine Learning (ML) and am also well-versed in Android development. I believe ML and Android together can provide impactful solutions for society.

I am actively seeking full-time opportunities in Software Engineering.

My family is a major part of who I am.

I owe everything to my parents. All my positive traits and achievements are the reason of their upbringing and values they have instilled in me. I am where I am because of them and this portfolio wouldn't be complete without thanking them.
